# fynk — Product & Positioning Context

## What is fynk?

fynk is an AI contract management platform for creating, sending, and legally signing documents. It helps legal, finance, sales, and operations teams automate document workflows and capture legally binding e-signatures with a full audit trail.

**Important:** "fynk" is always written in all lowercase — in code, UI strings, descriptions, README, and any other user-facing text.

## Target users

- Legal teams
- Finance teams
- Sales teams
- Operations teams

## Key capabilities

- Create documents from templates with dynamic fields
- Send documents to parties for review and signing
- Capture legally binding e-signatures: **SES** (Simple), **AES** (Advanced), **QES** (Qualified Electronic Signature)
- Full tamper-proof audit trail for every document

## Use cases

Users of this n8n node are likely automating workflows around:

- **Proposals** and **offers** — triggered when a deal reaches a stage in a CRM
- **Sales contracts** and **sales purchase agreements** — generated from CRM or ERP data
- **NDAs** (non-disclosure agreements)
- **Data privacy agreements (DPA)**
- **Service agreements**
- **Onboarding agreements** and **subscription renewals** — high-volume, repetitive documents
- Any document requiring a legally binding e-signature with an audit trail
